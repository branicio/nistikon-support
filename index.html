Nistikon

Today's fast, feasts & saints, Pascha countdown, and your prayer rule — bundled for offline use.

About

Nistikon is the daily companion for Orthodox Christians. Open it to see today's fasting rule in plain English, the feasts and saints commemorated, a countdown to Pascha, and your own Morning and Evening prayer rule. Bundled content works offline; the developer collects no personal data and shows no ads.

Features

  • Today's fasting discipline with a plain-English explanation
  • A 50-year color-coded fasting calendar (2026–2075)
  • Feasts, saints, and Scripture reading references for every day
  • New- and Old-Calendar modes for every major jurisdiction
  • A personal prayer rule from public-domain prayers, with reminders
  • Home-screen and lock-screen widgets

Frequently Asked Questions

Why doesn't the fasting rule match my parish exactly?

Fasting practice varies by jurisdiction, parish, and household. Nistikon follows a widely published simplification. Always follow the guidance of your own priest.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. The fasting calendar, saints, and prayers are bundled and work in Airplane Mode. Apple StoreKit may contact the App Store for purchases or restores, and links you choose to open use your browser or the App Store.

How do I switch to the Old (Julian) Calendar?

In Settings, choose a jurisdiction such as ROCOR or Serbian. Old-Calendar mode is part of Nistikon Premium.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open your device's Settings, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and select Nistikon.
