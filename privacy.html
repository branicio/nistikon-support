Privacy Policy

Nistikon — Orthodox Fasting Calendar & Prayer Rule
Effective Date: July 2026
Our Commitment: The developer of Nistikon collects no personal data. There are no accounts, analytics, ads, or developer-operated servers. Bundled calendar and prayer content works offline on your device.

Information We Collect

Nistikon does not collect any personal information. The app has no user accounts. The fasting calendar, saints, and prayers are bundled into the app and read directly from your device. Apple may communicate with the App Store when you load, purchase, or restore subscriptions. Legal, support, and App Store links that you choose to open leave the app and are handled by Apple or your browser.

  • Prayer Rule & Settings: Your prayer-rule selections, jurisdiction, appearance, and reminder preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • No usage data: We do not record which screens you view, which prayers you read, or how you fast.

Notifications

Nistikon can send optional local reminders for your prayer rule and for fasting days. These notifications are:

  • Scheduled and delivered entirely on your device
  • Local notifications only — no push server is involved
  • Fully controlled by your device's notification settings, and off by default

Third-Party Services

Nistikon uses no third-party SDKs, external content APIs, analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. There are no crash reporters or behavioral trackers of any kind. Purchases use Apple's StoreKit and App Store services.

Subscriptions

Nistikon offers an optional premium subscription (monthly or yearly). All subscription management and payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never receive your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Nistikon is rated 4+ and collects no personal information from anyone, including children.

Religious Content

Nistikon is a reference and practice aid. Fasting information is general religious guidance, not medical or dietary advice. Always follow the direction of your own priest or spiritual father.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
