The App

Nistikon is a reference and practice aid for Eastern Orthodox Christians, providing a fasting calendar, feasts and saints, a Pascha countdown, and a personal prayer rule built from public-domain prayer texts. Its bundled reference and prayer content works offline.

Religious Guidance, Not Rules

The fasting information in Nistikon reflects a widely published simplification of Orthodox fasting practice and will not match every jurisdiction or household. It is general religious reference, not medical, dietary, or pastoral prescription. Always follow the guidance of your own priest or spiritual father.

Content & Sources

All prayer texts are drawn from public-domain sources (notably Isabel Hapgood's Service Book of the Holy Orthodox-Catholic Apostolic Church, 1906). Saint descriptions are written independently for this app. No copyrighted liturgical translation is reproduced. Full attributions appear in the app under Settings › Content Sources.
